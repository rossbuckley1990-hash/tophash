THE VISION

TopHash is to structure what SHA-256 is to bytes.

A single mathematical primitive that makes every structured object searchable, identifiable, and provable.

01
A Structural Fingerprint
Training-free, deterministic, fixed-size. Fuses persistence, spectrum, and geometry into one self-tuned vector. Searchable in milliseconds across billions of structured objects.
02
An Exact Identity Layer
Canonical labeling + canonical serialization + machine-auditable proof objects. The canonical form is a complete invariant — equality is isomorphism, certified.
03
A Counterfactual Engine
Perturbation algebra + response tensor + invariant-core decomposition. Certifies what is invariant, what is fragile, and what is the minimal edit that flips a regime.
